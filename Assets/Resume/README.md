# How to add your Resume PDF

1. **Place your resume file** in this folder: `Assets/Resume/`
2. **Name the file** exactly: **`RESUME_2026.pdf`**

   The "Download CV" button on the portfolio already points to this file.

3. **Alternative name:** If your file has a different name (e.g. `Jatin_Bharmota_Resume.pdf`), you can either:
   - Rename it to `RESUME_2026.pdf`, or
   - Open `index.html`, search for `Assets/Resume/RESUME_2026.pdf`, and change it to your filename (e.g. `Assets/Resume/Jatin_Bharmota_Resume.pdf`).

**Full path for this project:**  
`d:\PORTFOLIO\NeoBrutalist-main\Assets\Resume\RESUME_2026.pdf`

Just drag and drop your PDF here and rename it to `RESUME_2026.pdf` if needed.
